Privacy Policy

Secure QR Share  ·  Last updated: March 6, 2026

Short version: Secure QR Share does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens on your device.

1. Overview

Secure QR Share ("the App") is a utility that lets you encrypt text into QR codes and scan QR codes to copy text to your clipboard. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • The text or passwords you encrypt or scan
  • Camera images or video

3. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera solely to scan QR codes. Camera frames are processed entirely on-device in real time and are never stored, saved, or transmitted anywhere.

4. Clipboard

When a QR code is successfully scanned or decrypted, the resulting text is written to your device's clipboard for your convenience. The App does not read from your clipboard.

5. Encryption

All encryption and decryption is performed locally on your device using AES-256-CBC. Your PIN and the text you encrypt never leave your device through the App.

6. Internet Access

The App does not make any network requests. The only internet-related functionality is the QR code URL format (https://revold1985.github.io/scan), which is a static web page hosted on GitHub Pages used as a fallback for users who do not have the App installed. No data is sent to this page.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
