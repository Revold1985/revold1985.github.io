Task Master — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026  ·  Developer: Revold1985

1. Overview

Task Master is a personal productivity and accountability app. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Task Master does not require an account and operates entirely offline except when using the AI verification feature.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Data stored locally on your device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

DataPurpose
Tasks (name, description, schedule, verification settings)Core app functionality
Task history (completions and failures)History screen and streak tracking
Points and streaksProductivity scoring
Accountability partner PIN (SHA-256 hash only)Verification; original PIN never stored

2.2 Photos — AI Verification

When you use the AI Verification feature, you take or select a photo to prove task completion. That photo is sent directly to the Anthropic API (Claude) solely to determine whether your task is complete.

Task Master does not store, retain, or share your photos. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy.

2.3 Location — Location-Based Verification

When you use Location-Based Verification, the app reads your device's GPS to check whether you are within a defined area. Location data is used only at the moment of verification and is never stored or transmitted.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All task data, history, and settings are stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device's Settings → Apps → Task Master → Clear Data.

6. Children's Privacy

Task Master is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
